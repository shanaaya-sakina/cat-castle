# Game Design: Fantasy Top-Down Adventure

## Overview
A top-down fantasy adventure game (like Zelda). Explore one big connected map, fight enemies, get stronger, and survive.

## Player

**Attacks**
- Melee: sword swing for close enemies
- Ranged: shoot (e.g. arrows) for enemies far away
- Dash/roll: quick burst of movement to dodge or close distance
- Magic spells: special attacks (e.g. fireball), probably uses mana

**Health**
- Health bar (hearts or HP)
- Healing items (potions/food) to recover health

**Progression**
- Find gear: better swords, bows, armor scattered around the map
- Level up: earn XP from defeating enemies, level up stats over time

## World
- One big connected map, free roam (no separate levels/rooms)
- Fantasy theme: forests, castles, dragons, magic

## Enemies
- Patrol + chase behavior: enemies wander on their own, then chase once they spot the player

## Goal
- No strict win condition for now — focus is exploring and fighting
- Can add an ending (boss, final goal) later

## Open questions for later

- How does mana work for spells?
- Where does gear/XP come from exactly (drops, chests, quests)?
- Map size and what's on it (towns, dungeons, secrets)?
